Gothenburg Coffee House

This local coffee shop suddenly found they were struggling to compete with the larger chains that kept arriving in the city.

Together we created a simple survey for their customers to understand their strengths and weaknesses, then created a communication plan to promote their awesome coffee and customer service, while working on reducing the weaknesses, without affecting their profit.

After 3 hours of work together, they had the tools they needed to implement the communication plan themselves, and increase their customer base over 30% in two months.
