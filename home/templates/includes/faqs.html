    An example: as a local cafe you have an amazing amount of wonderful, regular customers who you see on a weekly basis. They have been with you for years and you are very grateful. However, do you have a plan in place to encourage new customers in, should your regular customers stop coming in for any reason.

    It is so important to look after your regulars; but it is also important to find new ones too.
