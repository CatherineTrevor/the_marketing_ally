Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does it work?

    You sign up for an account, and purchase up to 3 hours maximum, either digital work, or a face-to-face meeting, within the Gothenburg area.

    When purchasing these hours you can access my calendar to see when I am available, and provide the chose of 2 timeslots that suit you. Project hours are limited to three hours for any single purchase and must be the same type ie. 3 hours face-to-face or digital.

    You receive a confirmation email (usually within a few hours) confirming the timed slot for your project hours. In the future this part of the process will be automated.

    Make sure to give a brief explanation of the project and work required, so I can get up to speed. If I have any questions, I will get in touch.

  • I think I need some help with marketing, but am not really sure...

    It's very normal to question whether you need a marketing strategy, plan, digital marketing etc! A detailed, lengthy plan is not always necessary, but it's worth evaluating your options.

    Take advantage of my free 20 minute consultation available to all new clients. Together we can discuss what help you might need, and then put together a solution. It allows us both to understand if we should work together and what I can do to help.

    It might be that actually there is a simple (and free!) fix available!

  • I am a sole trader, with a small client base - does everybody need a marketing strategy?

    This answer really depends on your business and what you want to acheive.

    I'm sure many people will disagree with me, but no, I do not believe that it is beneficial for every business to have a detailed marketing strategy, I do however believe all businesses would benefit from some sort of marketing plan, with goals in mind.

    An example: as a local cafe you have an amazing amount of wonderful, regular customers who you see on a weekly basis. They have been with you for years and you are very grateful. However, do you have a plan in place to encourage new customers in, should your regular customers stop coming in for any reason?

    It is so important to look after your regulars; but it is also important to find new ones too.

    Benefit from a free 20-minute consultation and we can chat about your personal situation.

  • What happens if the work doesn't take the full hour I have paid for?

    Without honesty I wouldn't have a business, therefore it is important that we are as upfront and honest with each other as possible.

    If it takes me less time to complete the work than you have paid for, you can select to receive a reimbursement to the value of the time not worked (terms and conditions apply), or you can select to have the time transferred to other project work, at a time to suit us both.

    Alternatively, come into the city and I'll treat you to Fika!
